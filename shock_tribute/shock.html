Legend was born in Brooklyn, developed musical talent in Tampa, Florida. Named Shock G in Queens, and formed hip hop iconic group, Digital Underground in Oakland, California.

Greg Jacobs, aka Shock G, Humpty Hump, The Piano Man, Butter Fly, Original Big Nose Rapper... damn, he will be forever missed.
